"Travel light, live light, be the light."

January 21, 2023| 5 comments

Travel has become my way of reconnecting with what truly matters. It's not about the fancy hotels or packed itineraries — it's about feeling the breeze in a new city, hearing stories from strangers, and waking up somewhere unfamiliar with a calm heart. I’ve learned that simplicity makes every journey richer. The less I carry, the more I experience. And every step reminds me that the world feels more like home when I carry peace within me.
